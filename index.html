About this project

Hi, I’m Fronema. I created this small independent fan project to make it easier to browse and orient within the large archive of Cracking The Cryptic Sudoku videos. I plan to continue developing and refining this site over time, so things may change as it evolves.

All puzzles, videos and related intellectual property belong to Cracking The Cryptic and the respective puzzle setters. This site does not host or reproduce any original content — it only links to publicly available material and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Cracking The Cryptic.

Huge admiration and thanks to Cracking The Cryptic, and to bakpao and Vebby for the public Cracking the Cryptic Catalogue spreadsheet that made this possible. Feedback is welcome at fronema@gmail.com.
