About

Hi, I’m Fronema and I created this web project to help navigate the Cracking the Cryptic Sudoku videos. It’s a fan project built out of admiration and a mild obsession with Sudoku — and I plan to keep refining and improving it over time, so things may change. Huge admiration to Cracking the Cryptic for their incredible work and the puzzles they bring to life. Many thanks as well to Bakpao and Vebby for the Cracking the Cryptic Catalogue spreadsheet that makes this possible. I don’t own any of the puzzles, videos, or related content referenced here. Everything belongs to Cracking the Cryptic and the individual puzzle setters. Feedback is welcome at fronema@gmail.com .
